Typography

Lexend is a typeface that was created to aid people with dylexia as well as reduce visual strain when reading. Lexend is a sans-serif font to reduce cognitive noise, has expanded scaling to improve character recognition and hyper-expansion of character spacing which reduces crowding. We have chosen Lexend as our primary and only font due to it's accessibility and structure. The structure of the font helps support our naturally calming app and aligns with our overall goals.

The typeface chosen for the wordmark is called "Raillinc" and it was chosen due to it's curvature and rounded serifs which matches the Tranquility Crane's logo. A serif typeface suited our app more as the rounded edges creates a feeling of safety in comparison to a Sans-Serif font with sharp 90degree edges.
