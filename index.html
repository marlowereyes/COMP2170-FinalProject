Tranquility Cranes is an app and companion designed towards those who need a helping hand to get through life’s chaos. We are BCIT students of the Digital Design and Development program, and we wanted to create an app that would help people through stress, anxiety and other mental health related issues. Our app contains various activities to do as well as a live chat both for community use and a urgent help line.

Meet Terry as he will be your personal guide and therapist throughout your experience on the Tranquility Cranes App. As the app is still in development Terry welcomes you to take a look around and explore the style guide!
