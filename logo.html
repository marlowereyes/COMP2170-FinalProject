Main Logo

The Tranquility Cranes logo takes the shape of the Yin and Yang with the combination of a crane bird. Since our app is designed to help people manage their stress and anxiety and bring tranquility/peace we decided to incorporate the Yin Yang and the crane bird because they are symbols of the essence of peace. The logo uses a lot of curves and avoids sharp edges because it's meant to give off a feeling of safely. Even in the typeface which is a serif, incorporates cursive writing and try's to avoid sharp edges. The main logo uses a blue and white gradient because those colors gives off a sense of calm and relaxation.
